Coping with a Disaster or Traumatic Event

OVERVIEW

During and after a disaster, it's common to undergo a range of intense emotions. Managing these feelings and reaching out for support is crucial for personal and community recovery. Stay connected with loved ones and your community, prioritize self-care, and recognize when it's necessary to seek assistance.
