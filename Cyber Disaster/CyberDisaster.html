CYBER DISASTER

A cyber disaster is a major event in cybersecurity that causes widespread disruption, damage, or loss, often through large-scale cyberattacks targeting critical infrastructure or data breaches compromising sensitive information. These incidents can have significant economic and social impacts, emphasizing the importance of strong cybersecurity measures and preparedness.
